Enterprise FAQ

How does HoloCraft integrate with existing enterprise systems?

HoloCraft Enterprise solutions offer comprehensive integration capabilities through our Enterprise API and SDK. We support integration with major enterprise systems including SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, and Microsoft Dynamics.

Our professional services team can develop custom connectors for proprietary systems and provide full implementation support to ensure seamless integration with your existing infrastructure.

What security measures are in place for enterprise deployments?

HoloCraft Enterprise implements robust security measures including end-to-end encryption for all data, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit logging. Our systems comply with industry standards including ISO 27001, SOC 2, and GDPR.

For organizations with specific security requirements, we offer custom security configurations and can work with your IT security team to implement additional measures as needed.

What kind of support is available for enterprise customers?

Enterprise customers receive dedicated support through our Enterprise Support Program, which includes:

- Dedicated account manager and technical support team
- 24/7 priority support with guaranteed response times
- Regular maintenance and updates
- Quarterly business reviews and optimization recommendations
- On-site support options for critical deployments

Can HoloCraft systems be deployed in secure or air-gapped environments?

Yes, HoloCraft Enterprise solutions can be deployed in secure, air-gapped environments with no external network connectivity. We offer on-premises deployment options that can operate entirely within your secure network.

For organizations with strict security requirements, such as defense contractors or financial institutions, we provide specialized deployment configurations that meet regulatory and compliance standards.

What is the typical implementation timeline for enterprise deployments?

Implementation timelines vary based on the scope and complexity of the deployment, but typically follow this schedule:

- Initial assessment and planning: 2-4 weeks
- System configuration and integration: 4-8 weeks
- Testing and validation: 2-4 weeks
- User training and rollout: 2-4 weeks

Our project management team works closely with your organization to develop a detailed implementation plan tailored to your specific requirements and timeline constraints.
