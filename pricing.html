Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for business purchases. For enterprise solutions, we also offer flexible payment plans and leasing options.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship our products worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. For specific information about shipping to your country, please contact our sales team.

For enterprise customers, we offer on-site installation services in select countries.

What's included in the warranty?

Our standard warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. It includes parts and labor for repairs, with shipping covered for the first 90 days.

Extended warranty options are available for all products, providing additional coverage and priority service.

Can I upgrade my HoloCraft device later?

Yes, we offer hardware upgrade paths for most of our products. Software upgrades are provided regularly through our update system at no additional cost during the support period.

For significant hardware upgrades, we also offer trade-in programs that provide discounts on newer models when you return your existing device.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for educational institutions, including schools, colleges, and universities. We also have specific educational packages that include curriculum materials and lesson plans.

Please contact our education team with your institutional details to learn more about our educational programs and discounts.
